About Us

Open Data

When Data Drinker started, Hearthstone data was controlled by a few large organizations. Our goal is to open meta-game analysis to the community, so that anyone with the inclination can assemble meaningful data about the state of the game.

To make this possible, 30for30 collects and releases game replays submitted by our contributors and collected through Hearthstone Deck Tracker.
