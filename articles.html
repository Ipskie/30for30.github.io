Hearthstone Data Discussion

Winrate in Hearthstone

In Hearthstone, winrate is king. Deck guides advertise it, tier lists are decided by it, even going infinite in Arena is tied to achieving a certain winrate. Winrates can seem like promises: if you learn this deck and play enough games, you too can win 60% of the time and earn that sweet legend card back

Uncertainty in Hearthstone

Plague of Wrath might not have been the best card. Actually, at the start of August, it was so bad that it’s Winrate was 0%. That’s right, exactly 0. HS Replay publishes their data to an astonishing 0.01% accuracy, so the card must have won less than 1 in 20,000 games.
