User Token

Each contributor is randomly assigned a token. The tokens help us group games by player, but (probably) aren't much use to you.

In the future, we may do giveaways or otherwise show our appreciation to our contributors, and might use the tokens then.

If you want to view your user token,

  • Click on plugins in the Hearthstone Deck Tracker app menu bar.
  • Click on "User Token" in the dropdown menu.
