FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

I play Hearthstone on mobile / my phone / tablet, can I contribute data?

Thank you for wanting to contribute!

Unfortunately, our collection system is built on Hearthstone Deck Tracker for Windows, which has an API and supports plugins.

If we develop a mobile solution, we'll let you know!

I have privacy concerns about contributing data.

Data Drinker does not collect any information about the player (i.e. you, the person).

We collect game data and metadata, and take steps to ensure it cannot be used maliciously.

We take the privacy of our contributors seriously, and have protections in place to ensure it is not breached.

For more information, please check our privacy page.

I tried to use the Data Drinker plugin, but ran into a problem. What gives?

We're sorry about that.

Please copy the error log (if any), and submit a [BUG REPORT].

I play infrequently / don't reach a high rank, should I still contribute data?

Absolutely!

We are focused on ranked Hearthstone played in both Standard and Wild format.

Arena, Dungeon Run, and casual games are not used in our analysis.
