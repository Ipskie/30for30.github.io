How does the plugin collect data?

Data Drinker uses the Hearthstone Deck Tracker (HDT) plugin API.

Decklists are received through the HDT program. Replay data is fetched from the HDT folder on your computer.

Our Privacy Philosophy

We collect only the data we need, and no more.

We will only share the data we receive if it benefits the wider Hearthstone community. This means no selling private data and no collecting without your knowledge.
