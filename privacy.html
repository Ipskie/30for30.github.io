Privacy

Data Collection & Protections

What data do you collect?

We collect the same .hdtreplay file as HS Replay, as well as your decklist, rank, and format (Standard vs Wild).

For detailed information on the data, you can check our docs.

How does the plugin collect data?

Data Drinker uses the Hearthstone Deck Tracker (HDT) plugin API.

We receive your decklist from hdt.exe, replay data is fetched from the HDT folder on your computer.

Curious contributors may examine the plugin source on Github.

How is privacy protected?
Random User Token

Each contributor is assigned a random user token. This is the only way contributors are identified: we will never (and can never) know your name, email, or other personal information.

Redaction

.hdtreplay files contain your battletag (Player#9001) and a game timestamp.
We redact your battletag and adjust the timestamp randomly (Â±18hrs), so that information your Hearthstone habits is masked.

What data do you share?

Your decklists, rank, .hdtreplay files, and a .json summary generated from those files will be uploaded to Dropbox and made available to the public through our data page.

If you do not want your decklist shared publicly (e.g. you are preparing for a high stakes tournament) or have concerns about privacy, you may disable Data Drinker from the plugin menu at any time.

Our Privacy Philosophy

We collect only the data we need, and no more.

We will only share the data we receive if it benefits the wider Hearthstone community. This means no selling private data and no collecting without your knowledge.
